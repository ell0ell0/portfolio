Meal Mobile

As part of an urban application design class I designed a food truck app called Meal Mobile. Meal Mobile is a location-based app that provides a convenient way for locating, discovering, and favoriting food trucks. I wanted to create a simple functional interface that could provide cart profiles, twitter feeds from the trucks/vendors, and reccomendations based on a users current location. Since this was a rapid prototyping exercise I stuck with the off the shelf IOS buttons and patterns and focused on organizing the content and functionality of the app.
