Thanks for stopping by!

I'm Arthur Lee Williams III. Yes, the third, which is why everyone has always used my middle name (Lee). I’m a designer who’s worked in magazine publishing a little internet startup and now the government. I’m jack of all design trades. I can sketch, wireframe, mockup, code, art direct a photoshoot, make motion graphics and create funky arduino electronic do-hickeys. I love doing it all and I think my portfolio reflects this.

When not working, I’m probably playing video games (PC ftw), working on my motorcycle compulsively, shooting (bow and arrow), playing table top games, and trying to find a bar to play shuffleboard at.

If you are interested in working together I'm available for freelance, contract or full time work.
